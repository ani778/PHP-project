AUDIT AND RELATED SERVICES

Audit of financial statements prepared in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)

We provide audit services to clients from a variety of sectors. To provide strong independent assurance to our clients we conduct audit in accordance with the requirements of International Standards of Auditing and adhere to the Code of Ethics for Professional Accountants in Public Practice. The high quality of our services derives from profound experience of our founding partners that comes from auditing wide range of outstanding international and local leading companies. In addition, comprehensive understanding of complex accounting and legislative issues enables our specialists to assist our clients in responding business challenges in dynamic market environment. Moreover, close ongoing cooperation with our clients allows us to keep them informed of relevant changes and issues regarding financial reporting standards and regulatory and legislative frameworks.

n order to deliver an efficient and effective audit to our clients we conduct detailed audit planning to identify and concentrate our efforts on areas of greatest business risks at early stages of audit. As well deep insight in many industries allows us to design and perform audit procedures that are tailored to our clients and to the best meet their financial reporting needs.

Review of financial statements

During review of financial statements we perform mainly analytical procedures and express opinion with negative assurance.

Agreed upon Procedures

During agreed upon procedures we perform our tests in the areas agreed with clients and provide report on factual findings

Compilation

During compilation services we compile financial statements of the clients in accordance with applicable financial reporting framework.
